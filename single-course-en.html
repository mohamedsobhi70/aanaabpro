What you will learn:

Delve into the core features of Microsoft Excel to harness its full potential. This course is designed to elevate your data analysis capabilities, ensuring you can confidently navigate and utilize Excel's vast toolkit.

Data Organization: Techniques for sorting, filtering, and grouping data.
Formula Fundamentals: Unlock the power of Excel formulas, basic and advanced.
PivotTable Proficiency: Summarize and represent data using PivotTables.
Advanced Analysis Tools: Explore forecasting with Goal Seek and Data Tables.
Data Protection: Learn to set entry rules and safeguard worksheets.
Macro Introduction: Get started with automating tasks through macros.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to your most pressing questions about the Consulting Foundation Program.

Who is the Consulting Foundation Program designed for?

The Consulting Foundation Program is tailored for a diverse range of individuals. It's ideal for fresh university graduates aiming to venture into consulting, young employees and experienced managers who aspire to refine their business acumen, government employees eager to adopt a consultant's mindset for enhanced collaboration, and essentially anyone keen on cultivating solid and adaptable business skills.
